What We Do

Our Mission

Christ-Centered Care for Children

We provide safe, loving, Christ-centered care for children up to age 14 who have experienced neglect or abuse. Our family-style homes offer safe havens where children can rest, live, learn, grow, and be deeply loved.

Support for Parents & Caregivers

We walk alongside parents and caregivers with compassionate care, comprehensive support, practical guidance, and a thriving Christ-centered community.

“Healed Children Heal The World.”

Our Vision

  • Children transformed by Christ To create a place where children heal, live, grow and learn how to be people who are led by the Holy Spirit; and grow to be disciples of Jesus.

  • Christ-centered spaces for caregivers to find care, support, guidance, and community.

  • Generational transformation where healed children become adults who bring healing to families and communities.

How We Live This Out

Daily rhythms and programs that make healing tangible.

Community Engagement & Education

The Kind of Children We’re Raising

Healthy + Whole — nurtured by a five-phase healing model.

The Healthy

Five-Phase Healing Model

Our trauma-informed, research-based approach guides children through five phases of healing—from safety and stabilization to integration and thriving. Each phase builds on the last, creating a foundation for lasting transformation.

Our Services

Flexible care options matched to each child’s needs.

Short-term relief care for parents and caregivers who need a break. Our respite care is provided at no cost and gives caregivers the rest they need to continue providing excellent care.

  • Up to 3 days of care
  • No cost to families
  • Safe, loving environment

Immediate placement for children in crisis situations. We provide safety, stability, and trauma-informed care while working with families and authorities to determine the best long-term plan.

  • 24/7 availability
  • Immediate trauma support
  • Care assessment & planning

Transitional care while families work toward reunification or while permanent placements are being arranged. We provide stability, therapeutic support, and educational continuity.

  • Therapeutic services
  • Educational support
  • Family reunification services

Extended residential care for children who need a stable, loving home for an extended period. We become family—walking alongside children through their healing journey and helping them build life skills.

  • Long-term stability & consistency
  • Comprehensive healing programs
  • Life skills development
  • Permanent placement planning

Note: The tier of care (respite, emergency, short-term, or long-term) is determined at the time of pickup/placement based on the child’s needs and family situation. We work closely with families and authorities to ensure the best care plan for each child.
