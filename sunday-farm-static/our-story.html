Our Story

Why “The Sunday Farm”?

A name that speaks to our heart, mission, and hope for every child.

Sunday

Sunday represents the first day of the week — a fresh start, a new beginning. It’s a day of rest, worship, and encountering Jesus.

For every child who comes to us, Sunday symbolizes hope — the promise that no matter what has happened, today can be the beginning of something beautiful.

Farm

A farm is a place of growth and cultivation. It requires patience, consistent care, and deep love. Seeds are planted, roots take hold, and with time and nurturing, beautiful things flourish.

This is where real, transformative healing happens — not rushed, but cultivated with intention, love, and the gentle guidance of the Holy Spirit.

“Here, children come in and are changed forever.”

And when children heal — the world heals too.

The Beginning

The Sunday Farm was born from a simple yet profound conviction: that every child deserves to experience the healing love of Christ, and that when children are healed, they become agents of healing in the world.

Our founders — a group of passionate social workers, therapists, counselors, and Christ-followers — recognized an urgent need in our community: children who had experienced deep trauma needed more than just a safe place to stay. They needed a home where they could encounter God’s love, experience true healing, and discover their identity in Christ.

What started as a vision shared around a kitchen table has grown into a comprehensive ministry serving children and families throughout our region. But our heart remains the same: to create spaces where healing happens, where Christ is encountered, and where children discover that they are deeply loved and have profound purpose.

Our Founding Philosophy

“We believe that every child — no matter what they’ve experienced — has the capacity to heal, to grow, and to thrive when surrounded by Christ-centered love and care. And we believe that healed children don’t just change their own lives — they change the world.”

Our Journey of Growth

  1. Phase 1: Foundation

    Planting the Seeds

    We opened our first home with a vision to provide Christ-centered care for children who had experienced trauma. Core programs and trauma-informed protocols were established by a dedicated team of caregivers.

  2. Phase 2: Growth

    Taking Root

    Services expanded to therapeutic support, educational programs, and family reunification. We added homes and forged partnerships with churches, schools, and community organizations.

  3. Phase 3: Expansion

    Bearing Fruit

    We launched parent & caregiver support, recognizing healing extends to the whole family system. Recruitment and training equipped more families to provide Christ-centered homes.

  4. Phase 4: Today

    Multiplying Impact

    We now serve children and families through residential care, respite services, therapy, mentorship, life-skills, and community engagement — creating spaces for children to lead, worship, and share their gifts.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our commitment to Christ-centered healing remains unwavering. We’re excited about the future — not because of our own strength, but because we serve a God who specializes in redemption, restoration, and new beginnings.

We’re grateful to participate in God’s work of healing children and transforming the world, one life at a time.
