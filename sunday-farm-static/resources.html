Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our services and programs.

1 How do I place a child in your care?

Call (555) 123-4567 or email info@sundayfarm.org.

Start Intake Process

2 What ages do you serve?

We primarily serve children up to age 14. Older children may be accommodated case-by-case. Caregiver support is available for all ages.

3 How do I become a foster parent?

The process includes an application, background checks, home study, and comprehensive training. We provide ongoing support and community.

Learn About Fostering

4 What services do you provide?

Residential care (respite, emergency, short-term, long-term), therapeutic and educational support, caregiver programs, reunification services, and community engagement.

5 Is there a cost for your services?
